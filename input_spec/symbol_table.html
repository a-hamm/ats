\(g\) gravitational acceleration vector \([m s^-2]\)

\(\kappa\) thermal conductivity \([MW m^-1 K^-1]\)

\(p\) pressure of the liquid phase \([Pa]\)

\(Q_e\) source of energy \([MJ s^-1]\)
\(Q_s\) source of solute mass \([mol s^-1]\)
\(Q_w\) mass source of water \([mol s^-1]\)
\(s_X\) saturation of phase X \([-]\)
\(t\) time variable \([s]\)
\(z\) elevation \([m]\)

\(\mathbf{V}\) Darcy velocity vector \([m s^-1]\)
\(\mathbf{V_s}\) surface velocity vector \([m s^-1]\)
